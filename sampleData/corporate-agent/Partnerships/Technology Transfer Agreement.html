Technology Transfer Agreement

Between BP p.l.c. and Petróleo Brasileiro S.A. (Petrobras)

Date: 12 April 2018

Recitals

Whereas, BP p.l.c. (hereinafter referred to as "BP") and Petróleo Brasileiro S.A. (hereinafter referred to as "Petrobras") have entered into a strategic alliance to explore joint projects and potential commercial agreements in various areas of mutual interest; and

Whereas, the parties intend to form a joint venture company (hereinafter referred to as the "Company") to carry out the activities outlined in the strategic alliance; and

Whereas, BP and Petrobras wish to transfer certain technologies to the Company to enhance its operations and competitiveness; and

Whereas, the parties wish to set forth the terms and conditions of the technology transfer through this Technology Transfer Agreement.

1. Definitions

1.1 "Technology" shall mean the proprietary technology, know-how, processes, formulas, designs, software, and other technical information owned by BP and Petrobras that are to be transferred to the Company.

1.2 "Transfer Date" shall mean the date on which the technology is officially transferred to the Company.

2. Technology to be Transferred

2.1 BP shall transfer the following technology to the Company:

  • Advanced drilling techniques and equipment.
  • Enhanced oil recovery methods.
  • Proprietary software for geological and geophysical analysis.

2.2 Petrobras shall transfer the following technology to the Company:

  • Refining processes and catalysts.
  • Downstream logistics and distribution systems.
  • Proprietary software for supply chain management.

3. Transfer of Technology

3.1 The technology shall be transferred to the Company on the Transfer Date, which is 30 June 2018.

3.2 The transfer shall include all necessary documentation, training, and support to ensure the Company can effectively utilize the technology.

4. Payment for Technology

4.1 In consideration for the transfer of technology, the Company shall pay BP and Petrobras a total sum of 10,000,000 Brazilian Reals (BRL), to be paid in equal installments over a period of two years, starting from the Transfer Date.

5. Intellectual Property Rights

5.1 The intellectual property rights in the transferred technology shall remain with BP and Petrobras. The Company shall be granted a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the technology for the purposes of its business operations.

5.2 The Company shall not sublicense, assign, or transfer the license without the prior written consent of BP and Petrobras.

6. Confidentiality

6.1 The Company agrees to keep confidential all information relating to the transferred technology, except where disclosure is required by law or regulatory authority.

6.2 The Company shall take all reasonable steps to protect the confidentiality of the technology, including but not limited to implementing appropriate security measures and restricting access to authorized personnel only.

7. Term and Termination

7.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Transfer Date and shall continue in effect until terminated by either party with 90 days' written notice.

7.2 Upon termination, the Company shall return all documentation and materials related to the transferred technology to BP and Petrobras.

8. Miscellaneous

  • Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
  • Amendments: Any amendments to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
  • Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter hereof.
  • Severability: If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
  • Notices: Any notices required or permitted under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been duly given if delivered personally, sent by registered mail, or sent by email to the addresses set forth below, or to such other address as a party may specify by notice to the other party.
