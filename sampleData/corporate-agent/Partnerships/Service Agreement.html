Service Agreement

Between BP p.l.c. and Petróleo Brasileiro S.A. (Petrobras)

Date: 1 May 2018

Recitals

Whereas, BP p.l.c. (hereinafter referred to as "BP") and Petróleo Brasileiro S.A. (hereinafter referred to as "Petrobras") have entered into a strategic alliance to explore joint projects and potential commercial agreements in various areas of mutual interest; and

Whereas, the parties intend to provide certain services to each other to enhance their respective operations and competitiveness; and

Whereas, the parties wish to set forth the terms and conditions of such services through this Service Agreement.

1. Definitions

1.1 "Services" shall mean the specific services to be provided by each party to the other as detailed in this Agreement.

1.2 "Service Provider" shall mean the party providing the Services.

1.3 "Service Recipient" shall mean the party receiving the Services.

2. Services to be Provided

2.1 Services Provided by BP to Petrobras

  • Upstream Services:
    • Provision of advanced drilling techniques and equipment, including training and support for Petrobras's technical team.
    • Access to BP's proprietary software for geological and geophysical analysis, with ongoing technical support.
    • Consultancy services on enhanced oil recovery methods, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Provision of expertise in health, safety, and environmental management, including training sessions and audits.
  • Downstream Services:
    • Access to BP's global network of suppliers and contractors, providing Petrobras with competitive pricing and reliable service.
    • Consultancy services on refining processes and catalysts, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Provision of expertise in downstream logistics and distribution, including training sessions and audits.
  • Trading Services:
    • Access to BP's trading platforms and market intelligence, providing Petrobras with real-time market data and analysis.
    • Consultancy services on trading strategies and risk management, including training sessions and detailed reports.
  • Low Carbon Services:
    • Provision of expertise in renewable energy projects, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Access to BP's proprietary technology for biofuels and carbon capture and storage, with ongoing technical support.
    • Consultancy services on low carbon initiatives, including training sessions and audits.

2.2 Services Provided by Petrobras to BP

  • Upstream Services:
    • Provision of refining processes and catalysts, including training and support for BP's technical team.
    • Access to Petrobras's proprietary software for supply chain management, with ongoing technical support.
    • Consultancy services on enhanced oil recovery methods, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Provision of expertise in health, safety, and environmental management, including training sessions and audits.
  • Downstream Services:
    • Access to Petrobras's extensive downstream logistics and distribution systems, providing BP with efficient and cost-effective distribution channels.
    • Consultancy services on refining processes and catalysts, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Provision of expertise in downstream operations, including training sessions and audits.
  • Trading Services:
    • Access to Petrobras's trading platforms and market intelligence, providing BP with real-time market data and analysis.
    • Consultancy services on trading strategies and risk management, including training sessions and detailed reports.
  • Low Carbon Services:
    • Provision of expertise in renewable energy projects, including site visits and detailed reports.
    • Access to Petrobras's proprietary technology for biofuels and carbon capture and storage, with ongoing technical support.
    • Consultancy services on low carbon initiatives, including training sessions and audits.

4. Term and Termination

4.1 This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue in effect for a period of three (3) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

4.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days' written notice to the other party.

4.3 Upon termination, the Service Recipient shall pay for all Services rendered up to the date of termination.

5. Miscellaneous

  • Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
  • Amendments: Any amendments to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
  • Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter hereof.
  • Severability: If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
  • Notices: Any notices required or permitted under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed to have been duly given if delivered personally, sent by registered mail, or sent by email to the addresses set forth below, or to such other address as a party may specify by notice to the other party.
