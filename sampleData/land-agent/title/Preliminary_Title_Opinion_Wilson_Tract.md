## I. SCOPE OF EXAMINATION

This Preliminary Title Opinion is based upon our examination of the following:

1. Abstract of Title No. 14352 prepared by Eddy County Abstract Company, covering the captioned lands from patent through July 31, 2023.

2. Certified copies of all instruments recorded in the records of Eddy County, New Mexico from July 31, 2023 through August 5, 2023, being the effective date of this opinion.

3. Information provided in the Drilling Title Opinion prepared by Smith & Garcia, LLP dated September 5, 2022.

This opinion is limited to the documents examined and expressly does not cover:

1. Rights-of-way, easements, or encroachments that would be revealed by a survey of the captioned lands.

## IV. REQUIREMENTS AND CURATIVE

1. **REQUIREMENT:** Obtain subordination of mortgage from First National Bank of Carlsbad to the oil and gas leases described in Section II.C.

2. **REQUIREMENT:** Obtain release of Mechanic's Lien filed by Southwest Fencing Co.

3. **REQUIREMENT:** Verify current marital status of James T. Wilson. If married, obtain ratification of lease from spouse.

4. **REQUIREMENT:** File certified copy of Letters Testamentary from Thomas R. Wilson estate in Eddy County records to confirm chain of title for James T. Wilson, Rebecca A. Wilson-Martinez, and Michael P. Wilson.

5. **REQUIREMENT:** Verify there are no unpaid federal estate taxes due on the Estate of Thomas R. Wilson.

## V. CONCLUSION

Subject to the requirements set forth in Section IV above, and based solely upon the examination of the documents described in Section I, it is our opinion that Permian Exploration Partners, LLC holds valid and subsisting oil and gas leases covering 100% of the mineral estate in the captioned lands.

Nothing herein shall be construed as an opinion concerning the validity or marketability of title to the captioned lands, and this opinion is subject to any additional information that may come to our attention.
