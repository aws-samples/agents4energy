## 1. GRANT OF LEASE

Lessor, for and in consideration of TEN AND NO/100 DOLLARS ($10.00), and other good and valuable consideration in hand paid, the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, and the covenants and agreements of Lessee hereinafter contained, does hereby grant, lease and let exclusively unto Lessee for the purpose of exploring, drilling, mining and operating for and producing oil, gas, and all other hydrocarbons, the following described land in Lea County, New Mexico:

**LEGAL DESCRIPTION:**  
Township 19 South, Range 32 East, NMPM  
Section 14: SE/4  
Section 15: E/2  
Section 23: NW/4

Comprising 640.00 acres, more or less.

## 2. TERM OF LEASE

Subject to the other provisions herein contained, this lease shall remain in force for a primary term of FIVE (5) YEARS from the date hereof and as long thereafter as oil or gas or other hydrocarbons are produced from said leased premises in paying quantities.

## 3. ROYALTY PAYMENTS

The royalties to be paid by Lessee are as follows:

(a) On oil, one-fifth (1/5) of that produced and saved from said land, the same to be delivered at the wells or to the credit of Lessor into the pipeline to which the wells may be connected. Lessee shall have the option to purchase any royalty oil in its possession, paying the market price therefor prevailing for the field where produced on the date of purchase.

(b) On gas, including casinghead gas, and all other hydrocarbons, one-fifth (1/5) of the market value at the well of the gas used or sold by Lessee, provided that on gas sold at the wells the royalty shall be one-fifth (1/5) of the amount realized from such sale.

## 4. DELAY RENTAL

If operations for drilling are not commenced on said land on or before one year from the date hereof, this lease shall terminate as to both parties, unless Lessee shall, on or before the expiration of said period, pay or tender to Lessor the sum of FIVE DOLLARS ($5.00) per acre for the number of acres then covered by this lease, which shall operate as a rental and cover the privilege of deferring the commencement of operations for a period of one year. In like manner and upon like payments or tenders, the commencement of operations may be further deferred for successive periods of one year each during the primary term.

## 5. POOLING PROVISION

Lessee is hereby granted the right, at its option, to pool or unitize any land covered by this lease with any other land, lease, or leases, as to any or all minerals or horizons. The units formed by pooling for oil shall not exceed 40 acres plus a maximum acreage tolerance of 10%, and for gas shall not exceed 640 acres plus a maximum acreage tolerance of 10%. In exercising its pooling rights hereunder, Lessee shall file of record a written declaration describing the unit and stating the effective date of pooling.

## 6. PUGH CLAUSE

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary herein contained, drilling operations on or production from a pooled unit or units established under the provisions of Paragraph 5 hereof embracing land covered hereby and other land shall maintain this lease in force only as to that portion of the leased land included in such unit or units. This lease may be maintained in force as to the remainder of the leased land in any manner provided for herein.

## 7. DEPTH SEVERANCE

At the end of the primary term or any extension thereof by production, this lease shall terminate as to all depths 100 feet below the deepest producing zone in any well drilled on the leased premises or on lands pooled therewith.

## 8. SURFACE DAMAGE PAYMENT

Lessee agrees to pay for damages caused by its operations to growing crops on said land. Lessee shall pay Lessor $2,500.00 for each well site location and $5.00 per linear foot for access roads. Additionally, Lessee shall pay Lessor $10,000.00 as a one-time surface damage payment prior to commencing operations.

## 9. SHUT-IN ROYALTY

If, at the expiration of the primary term or at any time thereafter, there is a well capable of producing oil or gas in paying quantities located upon the leased premises, but oil and gas is not being produced for lack of a suitable market, this lease shall not terminate if Lessee pays a shut-in royalty of $50.00 per acre for each acre then covered by this lease. Such payment shall be made on or before the anniversary date of this lease during the period such well is shut-in. This provision shall not maintain this lease in force for more than two (2) consecutive years.

## 10. CONTINUOUS DRILLING OPERATIONS

If, at the expiration of the primary term, oil or gas is not being produced but Lessee is then engaged in drilling or reworking operations, this lease shall remain in force so long as drilling or reworking operations are prosecuted with no cessation of more than sixty (60) consecutive days, and if they result in production, so long thereafter as oil or gas is produced from the leased premises.
