## Executive Summary

This report summarizes the financial performance and position of Permian Energy Corporation's joint venture operations for February 2024. The company currently participates in four major joint venture projects: East Permian Development (PERM-ALPHA), South Texas Eagle Ford (EAGLE-FORD-BRAVO), Northern Bakken Development (BAKKEN-GAMMA), and Gulf of Mexico Deepwater (GOM-DELTA).

Total joint venture revenue for February 2024 was $7,539,154, representing a 3.8% increase from January 2024. After deductions for royalties, taxes, and operating expenses, the net revenue was $4,695,969, yielding a net operating margin of 62.3%.

Key financial highlights for the month:
- Capital expenditures remained within budget across all joint ventures
- Operating expenses were 2.3% below budget forecasts
- Cash calls from non-operating partners had a 97.5% collection rate
- Total joint venture net cash flow was positive at $2.68 million
